Ingredients and Allergens

Pork (50%), Water, Rusk (contains Wheatflour, Fortified with Calcium Carbonate, Iron, Nicotinamide, Thiamin), Pork Fat, Pork Rind, Isolated Soya Protein, Potato Starch, Salt, Herbs, Dextrose, Stabiliser (E451 (i)), Preservative (Sodium Metabisulphite), Sunflower Oil, Flavouring, Antioxidant (E301), Spice Extracts, Acidity Regulator (E331 (iii)), Herb Extracts

For allergens see ingredients in bold

Handling and Cooking

Storage

Keep refrigerated, store between at 0°c to 4°c and use within 48 hours of opening or until the 'Use By' date, which ever is sooner. Suitable for home freezing: Freeze on day of purchase and use within one month, Defrost thoroughly before use and consume within 24 hours. Once defrosted do not re-freeze Do not cook directly from frozen. Best Before See Neck Tie

  • Suitable for Home Freezing
  • Keep Refrigerated

Usage

Always wash hands, work surfaces and utensils before and after handling raw meat.

Oven cook

FROM CHILLED: Appliances may vary, the following instructions are general guidelines only. Remove all packaging. Cook on a lightly oiled baking tray (sausage meat to be no more than 40mm deep) in the centre of a pre-heated oven for approximately 15 to 20 minutes at 200°c for conventional ovens (Gas Mark 6) & 190°c for fan assisted ovens (Gas Mark 5). Ensure product is thoroughly cooked and piping hot throughout. Do not reheat.
